Privacy Policy for Daraz Data Extractor

Last updated: [Date]

Data Collection and Usage

Daraz Data Extractor is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT track your browsing history
  • Does NOT share any data with third parties
  • Does NOT transmit any user data to external servers

What We Store Locally

The extension only stores:

  • ScraperAPI Key: Your API key is stored locally in Chrome's sync storage and is only used to make requests to ScraperAPI.com for web scraping purposes.
  • No other data is stored or collected.

Permissions Explanation

  • activeTab: Required to access and extract data from Daraz.pk product pages you actively visit.
  • storage: Required to save your ScraperAPI key locally in your browser.
  • downloads: Required to export scraped data as CSV/JSON files to your computer.
  • host_permissions (daraz.pk, scraperapi.com): Required to extract data from Daraz.pk and communicate with ScraperAPI service.

Third-Party Services

This extension uses ScraperAPI.com for proxy services when needed. Your ScraperAPI key is sent directly to their service for web scraping purposes only.
